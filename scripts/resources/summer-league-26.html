Summer League

The Summer Squash League 2025, subvented by the Leisure and Cultural Services Department, will be held from 9 June to 30 September 2025.

enrolment

The Summer Squash League 2025, subvented by the Leisure and Cultural Services Department, will be held from 9 June to 30 September 2025, and we are now accepting team applications.

This year's summer league has 10 divisions, including 9 divisions for the Main League and 1 division for Ladies.

 

Deadline of submission:  22 April 2025 (Tuesday)

regulations

venue

Due to the tight schedule, some of the matches will be scheduled on other nights, which differs from the original timetable. There will be certain weeks in which a team has to play 2 games within the same week. Convenors, please pay attention to the date and venue shown on the Schedule. 

 

Venue Information for Summer Squash League 2025

HKSC: Hong Kong Squash Centre 

Address: 23 Cotton Tree Drive, Central

 

CSPSC: Cornwall Street Park Squash Centre

Address: 17 Cornwall Street, Kowloon Tong

 

PHSSC: Pei Ho Street Sports Centre

Address: Pei Ho Street Municipal Services Building, 333 Ki Lung Street, Sham Shui Po

 

HRSC: Harbour Road Sports Centre

Address: 27 Harbour Rd, Wan Chai

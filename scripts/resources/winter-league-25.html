Squash League

enrolment

Hong Kong Squash League 2024/25, subvented by Leisure and Cultural Services Department, will be held from 2/10/2024. We are now accepting team applications.

The league will consist of Main League, Masters League, and Ladies League, which will be divided into 23 divisions.

Deadline of submission: 26 July 2024 (Friday)
